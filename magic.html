MAGIC: Michigan - Arithmetic Geometry Initiative - Columbia

MAGIC is an online seminar starting in May. The topic is arithmetic geometry, broadly construed to include parts of algebraic geometry and number theory, focusing on concrete problems.

The seminar is organized by Wei Ho (University of Michigan) and Will Sawin (Columbia University).

MAGIC meets Thursdays, 1:30 pm - 2:30 pm EDT, 12:30 pm - 1:30 pm CDT, on Zoom.

Using Zoom

The seminars will take place over Zoom. Some basics:

  • You can join for free by clicking on the link that we will provide below before each seminar. Only we (the hosts) need to purchase a Zoom license.

  • Please log in with your real name.

  • Audience microphones will be muted by default.

  • There is a text-based chat. If you would like to ask a question using your microphone, you can ask in the chat for your microphone to be turned on. Alternatively you can ask questions directly in the chat.

  • There is a 15 minute “coffee break” scheduled between the two talks.

We also ask for your patience with any technical difficulties.

Zoom link

The Zoom link will appear here each week, about 15 minutes before the start of the first talk.
