I am a world citizen. I was born in China, grew up between China, Germany and the US, I speak English, Chinese, and German.

In my previous life, I did science research and blogged about some science competitions I participated in, for instance Intel STS, JSHS.

I co-founded Young Partners for a Prosperous Athens, an anti-poverty youth empowerment group, while in high school.

When not coding, I enjoy reading, coding, and discussing politics and culture with my friends. I lived and was the treasurer at Rivendell Coop in Madison, a housing cooperative for people from diverse backgrounds. I run marathons in my spare time.
